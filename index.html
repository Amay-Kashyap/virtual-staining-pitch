Slide 3 | Workflow

We turn a 3-day lab process into a 5-minute bedside workflow

How SwiftDx Works

SwiftDx eliminates physical sectioning and staining by using autofluorescence imaging, virtual staining and AI-assisted analysis.

01
Optical sectioning of fresh tissue
The surgeon provides a fresh tissue specimen immediately after excision.
Collection
02
AutofluoroSense Imaging
The tissue is illuminated inside a dark imaging chamber. Surface fluorescence creates a virtual optical section approximately 2–3 μm deep.
Imaging
03
Virtual H&E Generation
AI converts the fluorescence image into a pathologist-familiar H&E representation without chemical staining.
Virtual Staining
04
Margin Intelligence Analysis
A vision-language model analyzes tissue morphology and identifies suspicious regions and margin status.
AI Analysis
05
Pathologist Review
The generated slide and margin report are reviewed by a pathologist who remains the final clinical decision maker.
Clinical Output
Physical Sectioning 0 No microtome required
Chemical Staining 0 No H&E processing
Turnaround Time <5m Intraoperative workflow
Output AI + MD Pathologist supervised
