Slide 2 | Problem
The bottleneck
37%

Positive margin rate in ovarian cancer surgery.

When tumour margins are positive, patients may need re-excision, radiotherapy, or chemotherapy. The surgical decision is urgent, but pathology still takes 24-72 hours.

Why now

Cancer case volume is rising while pathology workforce capacity stays constrained. The opportunity is decision support that speeds the queue without replacing the pathologist.

Slide 3 | Status quo
Current approaches break intraoperatively

The tissue is ready.
The pipeline is not.

H&E staining

Gold standard, but fixation, embedding, sectioning, and staining take 24-72 hours.

IHC add-on

Used when H&E is ambiguous; can add EUR3,000+ in reagent cost per case.

Manual review

Expert but scarce. Every case competes for the same specialist attention.

Frozen section

Helpful in select centres, but requires on-site pathology and still needs confirmation.

Q&A 2 | Data limits
Current drawbacks

Promising model.
Limited data.
Clear next bottleneck.

Dataset access

Today we do not have enough paired unstained tissue and final H&E ground-truth data.

Current PoC

The available public dataset supports bright-field / fluorescent staining, so the current model demonstrates fluorescent virtual staining rather than full clinical H&E.

Clinical gap

Before deployment, we need scanner diversity, pathologist-reviewed labels, failure-mode analysis, and prospective validation.

Q&A 6 | Technical depth
Architecture detail

Two models.
One clinical report.

Virtual staining

Pix2Pix/U-Net style image translation, about 31.4M parameters, generating stain-like output around 180 ms per 512px patch.

LVLM margin model

MONAI ResUnit with a vision-language head, about 86M parameters, focused on tumour-healthy boundary reasoning.

Output

Clear / close / involved label, confidence, evidence patches, heatmap overlay, and structured JSON for clinical workflow integration.

Illustrative console output: close margin likely at epithelial fold, confidence 0.91, supported by gland distortion, dense nuclei, and irregular interface across six patches.
